# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 17, 2026

MatchCal helps users browse FIFA World Cup 2026 fixtures and create calendar subscription links for selected matches.

## Data Collection

MatchCal does not ask users to create an account and does not intentionally collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

The MCP app processes the information needed to answer a request, such as selected teams, country or market, timezone, and excluded match IDs. This information is used only to generate fixture results and calendar links for that request.

## Cookies and Tracking

MatchCal does not use cookies, advertising trackers, or analytics in the MCP app.

## Calendar Links

Calendar subscription links may include match-selection options in the URL so a calendar app can load the requested fixtures. Anyone with the link may be able to see those options.

If you open a link in a third-party calendar provider, that provider handles the request under its own privacy policy.

## Service Providers

MatchCal is hosted on third-party infrastructure. Those providers may process technical request data needed to deliver the service, such as IP address, user agent, and request logs. MatchCal does not use this data to identify users or build user profiles.

## Children

MatchCal is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes

This policy may be updated if MatchCal's behavior changes. Updates will be published in this repository.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the repository owner through the GitHub repository.
